Meetily's AI Summary feature converts raw meeting transcripts into organized, structured summaries with key takeaways, decisions, action items, and more.

## Overview

After recording a meeting, you can generate an AI-enhanced summary that automatically:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Extracts Key Points" icon="list">
    Identifies the most important topics and discussions from your meeting.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Captures Decisions" icon="check-circle">
    Highlights decisions made and consensus reached during the meeting.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Lists Action Items" icon="tasks">
    Extracts actionable tasks and assignments with context.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Structures Content" icon="sitemap">
    Organizes information into logical sections for easy navigation.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Generating a Summary

There are two ways to generate summaries:

### From Meeting Details Page

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a Meeting">
    Click on any meeting in the sidebar to open its detail view.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Generate Summary">
    In the right panel, click the **Generate AI Summary** button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait for Processing">
    The AI will analyze your transcript (typically takes 30 seconds to 2 minutes depending on length and model).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review & Edit">
    The summary appears in a structured, editable format. Make any adjustments needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Auto-Generate After Recording

Meetily can automatically generate summaries when you stop recording:

1. Enable **Auto-generate summaries** in Settings
2. Stop your recording as normal
3. The summary will generate automatically in the background
4. Navigate to the meeting details page to view the result

  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Groq">
    **Fastest Option**

    - **Blazing fast**: Summaries in seconds
    - **Low cost**: Competitive API pricing

    - **Free tier available**
    
    Requires a Groq API key (free tier available).
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="OpenRouter">
    **Most Flexibility**
    
    Access dozens of models via OpenRouter:
    
    - **Model variety**: GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, and more
    - **Compare outputs**: Try different models on same transcript
    - **Unified API**: One key for multiple providers
    - **Competitive pricing**
    
    Requires an OpenRouter API key.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Summary Structure

Generated summaries include several key sections:

### Standard Sections

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Agenda">
    Main topics discussed during the meeting, extracted from the conversation flow.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Key Points">
    Important information, insights, or facts shared during the meeting.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Decisions Made">
    Explicit decisions, conclusions, or consensus reached by participants.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Action Items">
    Tasks, follow-ups, and assignments that came out of the meeting, including who is responsible when mentioned.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Closing Remarks">
    Final thoughts, next steps, or scheduling information mentioned at the end.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Custom Templates

You can create custom summary templates for specific meeting types:

- **Standup meetings**: Include blockers, progress updates, today's goals
- **Client calls**: Focus on requirements, feedback, deliverables
- **Brainstorming sessions**: Emphasize ideas, creative solutions, next explorations
- **Interviews**: Structure around questions, candidate responses, evaluation criteria

<Tip>
  Create and save custom prompt templates in Settings → AI Templates for consistent summary formatting across similar meeting types.
</Tip>

## Summary Actions

### Copy Summary
Click the **Copy** button to copy the entire summary as formatted markdown to your clipboard.

### Export Summary
Export summaries as:
- **Markdown (.md)**: For documentation, wikis, or note apps
- **Plain text (.txt)**: For simple text editors
- **PDF**: For sharing or archiving (coming soon)

### Regenerate Summary
Not happy with the result? Click **Regenerate** to generate a fresh summary with:
- Different model selection
- Modified prompt/template
- Alternative summarization approach

<Warning>
  Regenerating replaces the current summary. Copy or export it first if you want to keep the original.
</Warning>

## Custom Prompts

For more control over summary generation, use custom prompts:

1. Click **Custom Prompt** when generating
2. Add specific instructions like:
   - "Focus on technical implementation details"
   - "Highlight budget and resource discussions"
   - "Extract all customer feedback mentioned"
3. The AI will prioritize your instructions when generating the summary

<CodeGroup>
```text Example: Technical Meeting
Create a technical summary focusing on:
- Architecture decisions and rationale
- Technical challenges discussed
- Implementation approach agreed upon
- Performance considerations
```

```text Example: Sales Call
Summarize this sales call with emphasis on:
- Customer pain points and needs
- Solution fit and benefits discussed
- Pricing and timeline mentioned
- Next steps and follow-up actions
```
</CodeGroup>

## Collaborative Editing

Work with your team on meeting summaries:

1. **Generate baseline**: AI creates the initial summary structure
2. **Team review**: Share the meeting page link with team members
3. **Collaborative edits**: Multiple people can refine and expand sections
4. **Final version**: Save the polished summary to your meeting library

<Info>
  Summary changes are auto-saved as you edit. No need to manually save changes.
</Info>

## Summary Quality Tips

<Steps>
  <Step title="High-Quality Transcripts">
    Better transcripts produce better summaries. Use appropriate Whisper models (small or larger) for accurate transcription.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose the Right Model">
    - **Quick meetings**: Use Groq or local models for speed
    - **Important meetings**: Use Claude for maximum accuracy
    - **Complex topics**: Use larger models (Claude Opus, GPT-4)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Use Templates">
    Consistent prompt templates improve summary quality and formatting for recurring meeting types.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review and Refine">
    Always review AI-generated summaries. Add context, correct errors, and enhance clarity as needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Summary Search

Find specific information across all your meeting summaries:

1. Use the **sidebar search** to enter keywords
2. Results show matching meetings with context snippets
3. Click a result to jump directly to that meeting's summary

<Tip>
  Search works on both transcripts and summaries, making it easy to find discussions even months later.
</Tip>

## Performance Considerations

### Generation Time

Summary generation speed varies by provider:

| Provider | Typical Time (10min meeting) |
|----------|------------------------------|
| Groq | 10-20 seconds |
| OpenRouter (fast models) | 20-40 seconds |
| Claude | 30-60 seconds |
| Local (Ollama) | 1-3 minutes (CPU), 30-60s (GPU) |

### Processing Status

While generating, you'll see real-time status updates:
- "Processing transcript..."
- "Generating summary..."
- Progress indicators for long operations

<Info>
  You can navigate away during summary generation. The process continues in the background, and you'll be notified when complete.
</Info>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Summary generation failed">
    - **Check API key**: Verify your API key is valid and has credits
    - **Check model config**: Ensure model is correctly selected in Settings
    - **Verify connectivity**: For cloud providers, ensure internet connection
    - **Try different model**: Some models work better for specific content
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Poor summary quality">
    - **Use larger model**: Switch from base to advanced models
    - **Add custom prompt**: Provide specific instructions for what you need
    - **Check transcript quality**: Low-quality transcripts produce poor summaries
    - **Regenerate**: Try generating again, results can vary
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Summary is incomplete">
    - **Long meetings**: Very long meetings may be truncated (context limits)
    - **Model timeout**: Try a faster model or local processing
    - **Regenerate with focus**: Use custom prompt to focus on specific sections
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Local model not working (Ollama)">
    - Verify Ollama is installed and running
    - Check that the model is pulled: `ollama pull llama3`
    - Confirm endpoint in Settings: `http://localhost:11434`
    - Check Ollama logs for errors
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Privacy & Security

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Local Processing" icon="shield">
    When using Ollama, all AI processing happens on your device. Transcripts never leave your computer.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Cloud Processing" icon="cloud">
    When using cloud providers (Claude, Groq, OpenRouter), transcripts are sent via encrypted HTTPS. Providers may retain data per their policies.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
  For sensitive meetings (legal, medical, confidential business), use local Ollama models to ensure complete privacy.
</Warning>
