Meetily provides a comprehensive meeting management system that makes it easy to organize, search, and access all your recorded meetings.

## Meeting Library

All your meetings are automatically saved and organized in the sidebar:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Automatic Organization" icon="folder-tree">
    Meetings are listed chronologically with the most recent at the top.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Persistent Storage" icon="database">
    All meeting data is stored locally in SQLite with automatic backups.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Quick Access" icon="bolt">
    Click any meeting in the sidebar to instantly open its details page.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Smart Search" icon="search">

  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Creating Meetings

Meetings are created automatically when you start a recording:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start Recording">
    Click the record button to begin a new meeting session.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Optional: Name the Meeting">
    Give your meeting a descriptive title before or during recording. If not named, it will use the timestamp.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Stop Recording">
    When finished, stop the recording. The meeting is automatically saved.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Auto-Appears in Sidebar">
    Your new meeting appears in the sidebar immediately, ready to access.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Use descriptive meeting names like "Q1 Planning - Engineering" instead of generic names like "Meeting 1" to make them easier to find later.
</Tip>

## Meeting Details Page

Click any meeting to view its complete details:

### Left Panel: Transcript
- **Full transcript** with timestamps
- **Copy transcript** button for easy export
- **Scroll through content** with virtualized rendering for long meetings
- **Pagination** for meetings with 500+ transcript segments
- **Search within transcript** using browser find (Cmd/Ctrl+F)

### Right Panel: Summary
- **AI-generated summary** (if generated)
- **Edit and refine** summary content
- **Generate/Regenerate** summaries on demand
- **Export options** for saving summaries externally

### Top Bar Actions
<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Edit Title" icon="pencil">
    Click the meeting title to rename it inline.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Open Folder" icon="folder-open">
    Access the meeting's folder containing audio and data files.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Delete Meeting" icon="trash">
    Permanently remove the meeting and all associated data.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Search & Discovery

## Meeting Recovery

Meetily protects your meetings from unexpected interruptions:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Auto-Recovery on Startup">
    If the app crashes or is force-quit during a recording, Meetily detects incomplete meetings on next launch.
    
    You'll see a **Recovery Dialog** offering to restore these meetings.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Preview Before Recovery">
    Before recovering, you can:
    - View the transcript preview
    - Check the recording timestamp
    - Decide whether to recover or discard
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Automatic Cleanup">
    Recoverable meetings older than 7 days are automatically cleaned up to save disk space.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<Info>
  The recovery system saved transcripts every few seconds during recording, so even unexpected crashes lose minimal data.
</Info>

## Storage Management

Meetily automatically manages storage to prevent disk space issues:

### Automatic Cleanup

| Content Type | Retention Period | Configurable |
|--------------|------------------|-------------|
| Recoverable meetings | 7 days | No |
| Saved meetings | 24 hours after save | No |
| Completed meetings | Indefinite | Yes |
| Audio recordings | 7 days | Yes |

<Warning>
  Audio recordings are automatically deleted after 7 days to save space. Export important recordings before the retention period expires.
</Warning>

### Storage Locations

Meeting data is stored locally:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="macOS">
    **Application Support Directory**
    
    ```
    ~/Library/Application Support/Meetily/
    ├── database.sqlite (meeting metadata)
    ├── recordings/ (audio files)
    └── models/ (Whisper models)
    ```
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Windows">
    **AppData Directory**
    
    ```
    %APPDATA%\Meetily\
    ├── database.sqlite
    ├── recordings\
    └── models\
    ```
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Linux">
    **Config Directory**
    
    ```
    ~/.config/meetily/
    ├── database.sqlite
    ├── recordings/
    └── models/
    ```
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Meeting Operations

### Editing Meeting Titles

1. Click the meeting title at the top of the details page
2. Edit the text inline
3. Press Enter or click outside to save
4. Changes sync immediately

### Retranscribing Meetings

Reprocess a meeting with different settings:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Meeting Details">
    Navigate to the meeting you want to retranscribe.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click Retranscribe">
    In the transcript panel header, click **Retranscribe**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Options">
    Choose a different:
    - Transcription model (for better accuracy)
    - Language setting
    - Processing options
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait for Processing">
    The meeting is reprocessed with the new settings. This may take a few minutes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Retranscription requires the original audio file. If the audio has been deleted (after 7 days), retranscription is not possible.
</Info>

### Deleting Meetings

<Warning>
  Deleting a meeting is **permanent** and cannot be undone. All associated data (transcript, summary, audio) will be removed.
</Warning>

To delete a meeting:

1. Open the meeting details page
2. Click the **Delete** button (trash icon)
3. Confirm deletion in the prompt
4. The meeting is removed from your library

## Pagination & Performance

For meetings with extensive transcripts:

### Virtualized Rendering
- Meetings with 100+ segments use virtual scrolling
- Only visible content is rendered
- Smooth scrolling even with thousands of segments

### Lazy Loading
- Meetings with 500+ segments load in batches
- Scroll to bottom to load more segments
- "Loading more..." indicator shows progress

### Performance Stats
- View segment count: "Showing X of Y segments"
- Infinite scroll loads 100 segments at a time
- No performance impact even with 5,000+ segments

<Tip>
  For very long meetings (2+ hours), consider breaking them into multiple recordings for easier navigation and processing.
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Meeting not appearing in sidebar">
    - Refresh the sidebar by clicking the refresh icon
    - Check that the recording completed successfully
    - Verify backend connection (status indicator in sidebar)
    - Check application logs for sync errors
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Cannot open meeting details">
    - Ensure the backend server is running
    - Check for database corruption (rare)
    - Try restarting the application
    - Check that the meeting ID is valid
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Search not finding meetings">
    - Verify search query spelling
    - Try broader search terms
    - Check that meetings have transcripts (not just audio)
    - Rebuild search index (Settings → Advanced)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Slow performance with many meetings">
    - Enable pagination for long transcripts (automatic)
    - Archive old meetings by exporting and deleting
    - Increase system memory allocation
    - Consider database optimization (Settings → Advanced)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
