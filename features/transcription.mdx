Meetily provides fast, accurate, and completely private transcription using Whisper models running locally on your device. No audio ever leaves your computer.

## How It Works

As you record, Meetily continuously transcribes your audio in real-time:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Voice Activity Detection">
    Audio is analyzed to detect speech segments, filtering out silence and background noise.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Local Processing">
    Speech segments are sent to the local Whisper model for transcription.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Live Updates">
    Transcripts appear in the main panel as words are transcribed, with timestamps and confidence scores.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Auto-Scroll">
    The view automatically scrolls to show the latest transcription as you speak.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Transcription Models

Meetily supports multiple Whisper model sizes, allowing you to balance speed and accuracy:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Tiny">
    **Fastest** - Best for testing and quick notes
    - Size: ~75 MB
    - Speed: Very fast
    - Accuracy: Basic
    - Use case: Quick meetings, draft notes
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Base">
    **Balanced** - Good for most meetings
    - Size: ~145 MB
    - Speed: Fast
    - Accuracy: Good
    - Use case: General meetings, conversations
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Small">
    **Recommended** - Excellent balance
    - Size: ~466 MB
    - Speed: Moderate
    - Accuracy: Very good
    - Use case: Important meetings, presentations
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Medium">
    **High Accuracy** - Best for critical content
    - Size: ~1.5 GB
    - Speed: Slower
    - Accuracy: Excellent
    - Use case: Legal, medical, detailed discussions
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Large">
    **Maximum Accuracy** - Professional grade
    - Size: ~3 GB
    - Speed: Slowest
    - Accuracy: Best possible
    - Use case: Transcripts for publication, archival
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Info>
  You can change the transcription model in **Settings → Transcription**. The model will be downloaded automatically on first use.
</Info>

## Real-Time Transcription View

The transcript panel shows your live transcription with helpful features:

### Timestamps
Every transcript segment includes a timestamp showing when it was spoken (e.g., `[02:45]`).

### Confidence Indicators
Hover over timestamps to see transcription confidence scores:
- **High confidence** (90%+): Green indicator
- **Medium confidence** (70-89%): Yellow indicator  
- **Low confidence** (less than 70%): Red indicator

<Tip>
  Low confidence scores often indicate unclear audio, background noise, or technical jargon. Consider reviewing these segments manually.
</Tip>

### Text Cleaning
Meetily automatically removes filler words like "uh", "um", "er", and "hmm" for cleaner, more readable transcripts.

### Auto-Scroll
The transcript view automatically scrolls to show new content as it appears. Scroll up to review earlier content - auto-scroll pauses when you're not at the bottom.

For non-English meetings:

1. Click the **Language** button in the transcript panel header
2. Select your preferred language

## Post-Recording Processing

After stopping a recording:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Processing Transcripts">
    Meetily processes any remaining audio segments that were queued during recording.
    
    You'll see a "Processing transcripts..." overlay with progress.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Saving Meeting">
    The complete transcript and audio are saved to the local database.
    
    A "Saving meeting..." overlay appears briefly.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Ready to View">
    The meeting appears in your sidebar and you can navigate to it to view the full transcript and generate AI summaries.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Virtualized Rendering

For long meetings with hundreds or thousands of transcript segments:

- Meetily uses virtualized rendering to maintain smooth performance
- Only visible segments are rendered to the DOM
- Seamless scrolling through thousands of transcript lines
- Automatic pagination for meetings with 500+ segments

## Recovery & Reliability

Meetily protects your transcripts from unexpected interruptions:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Auto-Save">
    Transcripts are saved continuously to IndexedDB as they're generated. Even if the app crashes, your transcripts are preserved.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Recovery Dialog">
    If the app detects incomplete meetings (from crashes or force-quits), you'll see a recovery dialog on next launch offering to restore them.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Transcript Preview">
    Before recovering, you can preview the transcript to verify it's the meeting you want to restore.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Retranscription

Made a mistake with model selection? You can retranscribe any meeting:

1. Open the meeting details page
2. Click **Retranscribe** in the transcript panel header
3. Select a different model or language
4. The meeting will be reprocessed with the new settings

<Warning>
  Retranscription replaces the existing transcript. Make sure to export the current transcript if you want to keep it.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Transcription is slow">
    - Switch to a smaller model (base or tiny)
    - Verify GPU acceleration is enabled
    - Check CPU/GPU usage in Activity Monitor/Task Manager
    - Close other resource-intensive applications
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="No transcripts appearing">
    - Verify the Whisper model is loaded (check Settings)
    - Ensure audio is being captured (check recording bars)
    - Try speaking louder or closer to microphone
    - Check that VAD (Voice Activity Detection) is working
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Poor accuracy">
    - Upgrade to a larger model (small → medium → large)
    - Specify the correct language in Language Settings
    - Improve audio quality (better microphone, quieter environment)
    - Check that system audio isn't overwhelming microphone input
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Model loading failed">
    - Check internet connection (required for first download)
    - Verify sufficient disk space (models range from 75MB to 3GB)
    - Try deleting and re-downloading the model
    - Check application logs for specific error messages
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
